THiS CRM v0.11.0 - Intake Form Foundation

Upload checklist

1. Preserve the netlify/ folder.
2. Preserve the public/ folder.
3. Upload/commit all files from this ZIP to the GitHub repo root.
4. Confirm Netlify environment variables remain set:
   - NETLIFY_DATABASE_URL
   - CRM_ACCESS_TOKEN (temporary fallback until removed)
5. Deploy through Netlify with Clear cache and deploy if possible.
6. Confirm adviser login still works.
7. Confirm the new Intake tab appears in the main navigation.
8. Open `/intake` in a new browser tab and submit a test enquiry.
9. Return to the CRM Intake tab and confirm the enquiry appears with flags and raw assessment details.
10. Assign an adviser, add assessment notes, recommended pathway and consultation outcome, then save.
11. Convert the test enquiry to a client and confirm a new Client record opens.
12. Confirm the converted intake record is linked and cannot be deleted from the Intake workspace.
13. Spot-check Library, Clients, Dashboard, Tasks, Calendar and Billing still open normally.

Notes:
- This is the first road-testing version of the pre-client intake workflow.
- Intake records are deliberately separate from live client records until conversion.
- No file uploads are included in this version.
- New migration added only: netlify/database/migrations/202606020001_add_intake_enquiries.sql.
