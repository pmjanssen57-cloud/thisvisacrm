THiS CRM v0.9.0 upload checklist

1. Unzip this package.
2. Copy all contents into the GitHub repo root.
3. Preserve the netlify/ and public/ folders.
4. Do not edit previously-applied migration files.
5. Commit and push to GitHub.
6. In Netlify, use Clear cache and deploy site.
7. Confirm Netlify Identity remains enabled and invite-only.
8. Confirm CRM loads after login.
9. Confirm Library tab appears and a test Library item can save/delete.
10. Confirm Tools > Currency converts without the browser Failed to fetch message.
