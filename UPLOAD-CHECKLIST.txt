THiS CRM v0.10.5 upload checklist

1. Preserve the netlify/ folder.
2. Preserve the public/ folder.
3. Upload/commit all files from this ZIP to the GitHub repo.
4. Confirm Netlify environment variables remain set:
   - NETLIFY_DATABASE_URL
   - CRM_ACCESS_TOKEN (temporary fallback until removed)
5. Deploy through Netlify with Clear cache and deploy if possible.
6. Confirm adviser login still works.
7. Open an existing client and expand Client Portal.
8. Upload one small PDF under Portal PDFs and standard forms.
9. Set the title/category/note and confirm it is visible to the client.
10. Publish the portal update.
11. Open /portal in a private/incognito browser, log in as the client and confirm the Forms and instructions section shows the PDF.
12. Open the PDF from the client portal.
13. Confirm existing portal functions still work: notes/actions, billing milestones, documents required, dates, appointments and tools.
14. Test Clients, Calendar, Library, Tools and Print Profile after deploy.

Notes:
- Portal PDFs are limited to 4 MB each in this release.
- PDF files are stored in Netlify Blobs and metadata is stored in Netlify Database/Postgres.
