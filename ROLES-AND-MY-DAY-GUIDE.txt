THiS CRM v0.13.35 - Roles and My Day
=====================================

CRM roles
---------
The CRM now uses two adviser access roles:

Admin
- Full operational CRM access.
- Adviser profile and role management.
- Backup Centre access.
- Contact and Mailchimp-consent CSV exports.
- Can create new adviser profiles.

User
- Full access to operational CRM workspaces, including My day, Dashboard,
  Tasks, Clients, Enquiries & Intake, Bookings, Calendar, Billing and Library.
- Cannot open Adviser management or Backup Centre.
- Cannot download the contact-register or Mailchimp CSV exports.

Assigning a role
----------------
1. Sign in as an Admin.
2. Open Advisers.
3. Confirm the adviser Login Email matches their Netlify Identity login email.
4. Set CRM access role to Admin or User.
5. Save the adviser profile.

At least one active adviser must remain an Admin. The CRM blocks an attempt to
remove or deactivate the final active administrator.

First deployment
----------------
Existing adviser rows are initially set to User. Until the first Admin is
explicitly assigned, a logged-in user whose email matches an adviser profile is
treated as a bootstrap administrator so the roles can be configured. Assign at
least one Admin immediately after deployment.

My day
------
My day is now the first page shown after a successful login. It includes:
- Overdue and due-today action counts.
- New enquiry count.
- Consultations scheduled for today.
- A concise action list.
- Direct cards for the main CRM workspaces.
- Recently opened clients.
- Admin shortcuts for administrators only.

My day remains available as the first item in the desktop and mobile navigation.
The existing Dashboard remains the detailed workload-management view.
