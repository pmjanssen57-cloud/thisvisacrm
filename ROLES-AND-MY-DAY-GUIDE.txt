THiS CRM v0.13.37 - Roles and My Day Briefing Overlay
=====================================================

CRM roles
---------
The CRM uses two adviser access roles:

Admin
- Full operational CRM access.
- Adviser profile and role management.
- Backup Centre access.
- Contact and Mailchimp-consent CSV exports.
- Can create new adviser profiles.

User
- Full access to operational CRM workspaces, including Dashboard, Tasks,
  Clients, Enquiries & Intake, Bookings, Calendar, Billing and Library.
- Cannot open Adviser management or Backup Centre.
- Cannot download contact-register or Mailchimp CSV exports.

Assigning a role
----------------
1. Sign in as an Admin.
2. Open Advisers.
3. Confirm the adviser Login Email matches their Netlify Identity login email.
4. Set CRM access role to Admin or User.
5. Save the adviser profile.

At least one active adviser must remain an Admin.

My Day briefing overlay
-----------------------
My Day opens automatically after login as a focused layer above the normal CRM.
The Dashboard is loaded underneath and remains visibly present, but dimmed, until
My Day is closed.

The overlay includes:
- Overdue and due-today work counts.
- New enquiry count.
- Consultations scheduled for today.
- A concise list of work requiring immediate attention.
- Today's consultation details.
- Recently opened client files.
- Direct shortcuts into the main CRM workspaces.
- An adviser / whole-practice scope selector.

Use Enter CRM to close the overlay and reveal the Dashboard. A shortcut can also
open a specific workspace directly. Click outside the briefing, use the close
button, or press Escape to dismiss it.

Inside the CRM, use the My Day button in the desktop header or the My Day item in
mobile navigation to reopen the briefing above the current workspace. Opening My
Day does not navigate away from that workspace or discard unsaved edits.
