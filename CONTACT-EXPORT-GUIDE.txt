THiS CRM v0.13.36 - CONTACT EXPORT GUIDE

Location:
Enquiries & Intake > Export contacts

EXCEL CONTACT REGISTER
1. New contacts since last export
   - Exports only email groups with a new contact-form or intake submission after the previous Excel export.
   - The first use on a browser exports all matching records and establishes the baseline.

2. All contacts
   - Exports the complete retained register.
   - Establishes a fresh baseline for future incremental exports.

MAILCHIMP CONSENT CSV
1. New consented contacts since last export
   - Exports only full-assessment contacts whose latest recorded marketing consent was submitted after the previous Mailchimp CSV export.

2. All consented contacts
   - Exports the complete retained consent list.
   - Establishes a fresh Mailchimp baseline.

RESET EXPORT HISTORY
Clears both local baselines. The next incremental downloads will include all matching retained records.

IMPORTANT
Export history is tracked separately for the current login, adviser view and browser. It does not automatically follow the user to another device or browser.


Administrator access
--------------------
The Export contacts menu is available only to adviser profiles with the Admin CRM access role.
