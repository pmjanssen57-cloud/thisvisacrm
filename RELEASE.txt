v0.13.46 - Streamlined Adviser Interface

This release applies the four interface simplification passes together while preserving the existing CRM data model and workflows.

Dashboard and navigation
- Reframes the Dashboard as a daily attention view rather than a whole-system summary.
- Shows four primary signals only: due today, overdue, new enquiries and today's appointments.
- Removes the full client workload, billing totals and adviser snapshot from the Dashboard.
- Keeps portal notes, clients without next actions, critical dates and recent clients in compact secondary panels.
- Reduces the main navigation to Dashboard, Tasks, Clients, Enquiries and Bookings.
- Groups Commercial, Calendar and Billing under a Practice menu.
- Simplifies the global work-view and client-search toolbar.

Client list
- Replaces dense client cards with a compact work-queue row.
- Shows client name, matter type, current stage, next action, adviser and backup marker only.
- Removes OneLaw, SharePoint, strategy and progress detail from the list view.
- Retains adviser, matter-type and backup-matter filters behind a single Filters control.

Client record
- Keeps the client record summary-first and reduces the visible workspace sections.
- Makes Overview, Actions, Documents, Progress and Key dates the primary sections.
- Places Portal, Billing, Family and Notes & strategy under a More sections disclosure.
- Hides the save-status bar when the record is clean and no message needs attention.
- Reduces the overview statistics and places core contact/allocation fields in a collapsed disclosure.
- Hides empty optional snapshot items such as portal and appointments.

Visual polish
- Reduces shadows, borders, badge size and competing card treatments in the adviser workflow.
- Uses quieter navigation, list rows and panels with more consistent spacing.
- Retains responsive mobile and tablet layouts.

No database migration, dependency or backend API change is required.
